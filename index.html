About Run4Ricky

Run4Ricky is a foundation honoring Ricky’s legacy by promoting awareness and inclusion for individuals with disabilities. We believe in a future where everyone can thrive, no matter their ability.

Our Mission

Through advocacy, education, and community events, we aim to create equal opportunities and support networks for disabled individuals and their families.

Ricky's Story

Ricky was a bright, kind, and spirited individual who inspired everyone around him. Born with a rare condition, Ricky never let it define him. His courage, love for running, and passion for community brought people together in powerful ways.

The Run4Ricky Foundation was created to honor his legacy and continue the mission he started—to uplift, support, and celebrate the strength in all of us. Through every step, we carry Ricky's spirit forward—fueling change and promoting inclusion, one stride at a time.
